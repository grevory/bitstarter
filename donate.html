What Ultimate League?

Ultimate League is application for ultimate frisbee organizers. It is meant to be used for leagues and tournaments. There are not an abundance of quality league management apps available for ultimate frisbee organizers so Ultimate League is looking to fill that void.

The idea is for ultimate leagues across North America to have access to a system that will allow any league to have a beautiful website for free.

Our system will be built for mobile first since everyone has their phone on the sideline of a game.

We will handle 100% of the payment on the organizers behalf. No one likes to waste time meddling with payment transactions when there are so many things that require the league organizers attention. We automate the entire process from acception money to issuing refunds.

Speaking of automating, we want to automate scheduling. It has to cause the most headaches so let us randomize a schedule for the season and then you can mess with the dates to get the schedule to work exactly how you need it to work.

Planned Features

  1. Mobile-first design
    This app will be a responsive website with its important features easily accessible via phone.
  2. Beautiful interface
    Nothing is more annoying that trying to find some quick stats or where your next game is. This app will be built with the ultimate player in mind.
  3. Automated scheduling
    Scheduling games and avoiding conflicts can be a real headache for organizers. This app will attempt to randomize a schedule for you. Organizers will be able to make adjustments as necessary.
  4. League/Tournament-specific rankings
    Some leagues use plus/minus. Some use goals for follow by desc goals against. Some have spirit calculators that directly affect rankings. We'll accomodat everyone.
  5. Payment processing
    League and tournament organizers have enough on their plate without having to manage every individual payment transaction. This app will automatically handle all payment. Including refunds!
  6. API access
    Build your own website on top of this app.
  7. FREE
    Get started in minutes for free!

Why should I care?

If you have no interest in ultimate you shouldn't care. But I'm guessing if you read this far you have some interest in ultimate.

Here is why I care.

I was at a well-organized tournament in Toronto recently and each captain was afforded a printed copy of the schedule on a loose sheet of paper. Needless to say with all the moving from field to field a sheet of paper does not last very well, if it doesn't get lost along the way. I noticed that everyone was checking the schedule from their mobiles. And of course they would. The tournament had a website. The only problem was that the schedule was completely unreadable to a human being on a desktop computer and infitiely worse on a handheld screen. There was a obvious need for a mobile-friendly schedule.

Of course why stop there? Everything should be easily accessible by your phone.
